## 💻 Why do your Kakebo Online?

The traditional method invented by Motoko Hani in the early 20th century was based on physical writing. Writing down every expense by hand created a psychological connection with money that helped reduce compulsive buying.

However, in **2026**, carrying a physical notebook everywhere is not practical. This is where the concept of **Kakebo online** shines:

*   **Total Accessibility:** You can log that coffee or Netflix subscription from your phone the moment it happens, before you forget.
*   **Automatic Calculations:** No more calculators and errors when adding up supermarket receipts.
*   **Security:** If you lose your notebook, you lose your financial history. An online Kakebo keeps your data encrypted and always available.
